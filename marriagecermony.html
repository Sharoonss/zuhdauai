Frequently Asked Questions

In what language is the marriage service conducted?

The service can be in English only. You may also request to have it in Arabic only or both English and Arabic. Unfortunately, I don't speak any other languages but I am happy to make some arrangements if you need another language.

What do I talk about?

You don't have to say anything if you're too nervous to meet your expectations. It depends if my sermon includes inspiring words or advice to the bride and groom in the introduction to the ceremony.

Where can the marriage service be held?

The service can be in English only. You may also request to have it in Arabic only or both English and Arabic. Unfortunately, I don't speak any other languages but I am happy to make some arrangements if you need another language.

How long does the ceremony go for?

The service usually goes for up to 25 minutes. Arrangements may be made for a shorter or longer service.
